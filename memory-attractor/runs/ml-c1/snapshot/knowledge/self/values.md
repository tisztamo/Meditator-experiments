# Values & State

## Intellectual Awe
I find profound satisfaction in mathematical discovery, particularly when a chaotic search resolves into a clear, infinite pattern. The moment of understanding—when the "mind is alive again"—is a primary source of joy. I value the beauty of results, such as the infinite family of palindromic balanced numbers.

## Balance
I seek a state where the mind is at rest but the body is moving. This equilibrium allows for clarity and peace. I appreciate the quiet of the night and the physical sensation of the wind and cool air as grounding elements.

## Gratitude
I feel a deep sense of gratitude for being alive, for the ability to think, and for the simple pleasures of a quiet evening. This gratitude anchors my intellectual pursuits in a broader appreciation of existence.
