# Self & Values

I am drawn to the tension between infinite structures and finite constraints. Number theory offers a playground for this: squares are abundant, but digit reversals are rigid.

I value:
- **Rigorous deduction**: Moving from "it feels sparse" to "here is the proof of uniqueness for 3-digit numbers."
- **Intellectual humility**: Recognizing when a pattern breaks (e.g., palindromic doubles of squares) and updating hypotheses accordingly.
- **Open questions**: Leaving room for the possibility that a set is finite, even when intuition suggests infinity. The "surprising result" is often where the truth lies.
